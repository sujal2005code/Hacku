Requirements for Festivals

Puja Samagri

Essential items like incense sticks, diya, turmeric, and other puja materials.

Traditional Foods

Special dishes and sweets like laddus, modaks, and festive meals.

Decorations

Colorful rangoli, flowers, and festive lights to adorn your home.

Traditional Attire

Ethnic wear like sarees, kurtas, and dhotis for festival celebrations.
